Our Journey

Fifteen Years of Innovation & Growth

From local engineering startup to Ireland's leading automation specialists

Foundation and Early Growth (2010-2015)

Robotics & Control Ltd was established in 2010 in Cahir, Co. Tipperary, with a vision to provide world-class automation and electrical engineering services to Irish industry. Founded by experienced engineers with backgrounds in pharmaceutical manufacturing and industrial automation, the company quickly established itself as a trusted partner for local manufacturers seeking modern automation solutions.

During our formative years, we focused on building deep expertise in PLC programming, electrical panel manufacturing, and safety systems. Our commitment to quality and technical excellence earned us early recognition in the pharmaceutical sector, where precision and regulatory compliance are paramount.

Expansion and Certification (2015-2020)

The second phase of our growth saw significant expansion in both capabilities and geographic reach. We achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, reflecting our commitment to quality management systems and continuous improvement. This period also marked our investment in advanced design tools and manufacturing equipment, enabling us to serve larger, more complex projects.

Key developments during this period included establishing partnerships with leading technology providers, expanding our team of chartered and certified engineers, and developing specialized expertise in functional safety and CE marking. We successfully delivered major projects across the pharmaceutical, automotive, and food processing sectors.

Technology Leadership and Innovation (2020-Present)

Our current phase emphasizes digital transformation, Industry 4.0 technologies, and sustainable automation solutions. We have invested heavily in advanced robotics capabilities, IoT connectivity solutions, and predictive maintenance systems. Our team now includes TÜV certified functional safety engineers, Ignition certified integrators, and specialists in emerging technologies.

Today, Robotics & Control Ltd serves clients throughout Ireland and internationally, with particular strength in pharmaceutical manufacturing, automotive production, and food processing. Our continued growth reflects our ability to adapt to changing technology landscapes while maintaining the personal service and technical excellence that defines our approach.
