About Robotics & Control Ltd

Your trusted partner in automation, safety, and electrical solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Ireland, Robotics & Control Ltd began with a simple mission: to provide world-class automation and safety solutions that help businesses operate more efficiently and safely.

From our headquarters in Cahir, Co. Tipperary, we have grown from a small engineering firm to a leading provider of comprehensive industrial automation services across Ireland and internationally. Our journey has been driven by innovation, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we have successfully completed hundreds of projects across diverse industries including pharmaceutical, automotive, industrial manufacturing, and food & beverage sectors. Each project has strengthened our expertise and deepened our understanding of complex industrial challenges.
