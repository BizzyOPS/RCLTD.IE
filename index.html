About Robotics & Control Ltd

Robotics & Control Ltd is a leading Irish technology company founded in 2010, based in Cahir, Co. Tipperary. With over 50 years combined experience in industrial automation, electrical engineering, and safety systems, we deliver comprehensive solutions for automation and control of your processes across pharmaceutical, automotive, food & beverage, and manufacturing industries throughout Ireland and internationally.

As ISO 9001:2015 certified specialists, our team of chartered engineers, TÜV functional safety experts, and certified automation professionals provides turnkey project delivery from initial concept through commissioning and ongoing support. We combine cutting-edge technology with proven engineering methodologies to optimize operational efficiency, ensure regulatory compliance, and enhance workplace safety for our clients.

Expert Engineering

Our expertise spans electrical design, panel manufacturing, PLC programming, and electrical safety for pharmaceutical, industrial, automotive, food & beverage, and machinery businesses.
