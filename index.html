About Robotics & Control Ltd

Robotics & Control Ltd is an Irish technology company founded in 2010, based in Cahir, Co. Tipperary. With over 50 years combined experience, we offer advanced solutions for automation and control of your processes.

Our expertise spans electrical design, panel manufacturing, PLC programming, and electrical safety for pharmaceutical, industrial, automotive, food & beverage, and machinery businesses.
